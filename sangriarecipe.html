Red wine sangria with fruits

Spanish Summer Beverage

Ingredients assignment_turned_in









Instructions auto_stories

  1. Cut the apple and peach into cubes
  2. Cut 1 orange and lemon into slices
  3. Squeeze the juice of 2 oranges
  4. In a large jar, add half of each cut fruit (separate the rest). Add sugar and the brandy
  5. Using a wooden spoon, muddle the mixture into the bottom of the jar for a few seconds
  6. Add red wine, cinnamon sticks and the rest of the cut fruit
  7. Leave the jar in the refrigerator for at least 30 min to flavour well and freeze;
  8. At the time of serving, add ice, pour the sangria in each glass and. If you want, add some tonic water to make it more refreshing.
